Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness


We do not see any correlation between a city's latitude vs cloudiness.

However, a general observation is that most of the cities are cloudy during this time. This goes back to the fact that this analysis was performed in the month of August. In months during which the temperatures are extreme, more cities will map near 0 cloudiness.
